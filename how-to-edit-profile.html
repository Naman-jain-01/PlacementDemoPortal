How to Edit Your Profile

Follow these steps to update your profile information:

  1. Log in to the Placement Portal.
  2. Go to the "Profile" section from the dashboard.
  3. Click on "Edit Profile" to modify your personal details, resume, or academic information.
  4. Save changes and verify that the updates reflect correctly.
  5. Contact support if you face any issues during the process.
