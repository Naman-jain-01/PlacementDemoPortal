Application for NOC, Bonafide Certificate, or Recommendation Letter

Important Guidelines

In accordance with the Placement Cell notification dated 7th December 2024, all students seeking a No Objection Certificate (NOC), Bonafide Certificate, or Recommendation Letter for internships or employment opportunities are required to adhere to the stipulated procedure to ensure timely processing and issuance.

Download Application Format: Application Format for NOC.pdf

Procedure for Off-Campus Internship NOC/Approval
  1. Complete the Application Form: Duly fill the Application Format for NOC.pdf with accurate details and affix your signature.
  2. Submission of Soft Copy: Zip all the required files into a single archive and follow the Online NOC Request process through the Placement Dashboard. Please go through the instructions given below carefully for Online NOC submission.
  3. Supporting Documents:
    • For internship applications: Provide details of the job/internship opportunity.
    • For secured internships: Attach a copy of the confirmation email or offer letter.
    • If the organization specifies a custom NOC/Bonafide format, include it with the submission.
  4. Compliance with Academic Calendar: Verify that the internship duration does not conflict with the academic schedule.
  5. Specify Internship Mode: Indicate whether the internship is conducted virtually or on-site by selecting the appropriate option.
  6. Indicate Application Status: Specify whether the application pertains to a prospective or secured internship.
  7. Final Semester Internships: Off-campus final semester internships necessitate approval from the Head of Department, coordinated through the Placement Cell.
Additional Notes
  • Placement Cell Internships: NOCs for internships facilitated by the Placement Cell are pre-approved and processed in batches. A separate application is unnecessary unless the company requires an NOC without copying stu.poc@iiitg.ac.in.
  • Academic Registration: Only internships approved with an issued NOC will be registered with the Academic Section for curriculum credit.
  • Processing Time: Upon receipt of complete documentation, the Placement Cell will expedite the NOC issuance and notify you to collect it from the Placement Office.
  • Caution: Incomplete submissions may result in processing delays, particularly during peak periods such as December.

Online NOC Request Submission

Access the Placement Portal Dashboard using your institute email (e.g., username@iiitg.ac.in) and password to submit your NOC request electronically. Upon successful submission, an acknowledgment email will be sent, with copies forwarded to the Placement Office and Placement Cell, including you in the CC.

Access Dashboard
Required Form Fields
  • Company Name: Provide the name of the organization offering the internship.
  • Internship Duration: Specify the start and end dates (DD/MM/YYYY) and total duration (e.g., 3 months).
  • Application Type: Indicate whether the request is for a prospective or secured internship.
  • Internship Mode: Select virtual or on-site internship format.
  • Stipend Availability: Confirm whether a stipend is offered (Yes/No).
  • Additional Remarks: Include any relevant comments or details.
  • Document Upload: Optionally upload the offer letter and organization-specific NOC format as a single ZIP file, if available.
